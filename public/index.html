What You’ll Learn

This Lifeline Course walks you through how to clip in, trust your system, move with confidence, and stay safe while you climb. You’ll see real‑world breakdowns, canopy footage, and step‑by‑step explanations designed for both new climbers and working arborists.

Every lesson is short, focused, and built to be watched again — even when you’re already in the tree.

Video Lessons

Lesson 1 — Understanding Your Lifeline

Learn what your lifeline actually does, how it’s built, and why every connection point matters.

Lesson 2 — Clipping In & Trusting the System

Step‑by‑step clipping in, checking your system, and building trust before you ever leave the ground.

Lesson 3 — Basic Movement on Lifeline

Learn how to move, sit, and reposition on your rope without fighting your system or losing control.
